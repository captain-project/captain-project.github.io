Meet our team

Daniele Silvestro

Daniele Silvestro is a computational biologist at ETH Zurich, Switzerland and a research associate at Gothenburg University, Sweden.

Stefano Goria

Stefano Goria has a PhD in theoretical physics and is the CTO and co-founder of Thymia Limited, a London-based mental health tech start-up.

Thomas Sterner

Thomas Sterner is a Professor of Environmental Economics at the University of Gothenburg, His research and publications are mostly focused on the design of policy instruments which address environmental problems.

Alexandre Antonelli

Alexandre Antonelli is the Director of Science at the Royal Botanic Gardens - Kew and Professor at the Department of Biological and Environmental Sciences and Gothenburg Global Biodiversity Centre at Gothenburg University.
